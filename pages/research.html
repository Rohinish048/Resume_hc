Vellore Institute of Technology, Vellore, Tamil Nadu

First author of reseaarch paper, Temperature Prediction of Lithium-Ion Cells Using Transformers and Deep Learning Models

Jan 2024 – Apr 2025 (On-site)

Published and presented my research paper “Temperature Prediction of Lithium-Ion Cells Using Transformers and Deep Learning Models” as the first and main contributing author at the IEEE International Conference on Data Science and Business Systems (ICDSBS 2025), where it received the Best Paper Award. This 16-month project (Jan 2024 – Apr 2025) pioneered the use of Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS) with advanced architectures—including Transformers, LSTM, GRU, Bi-LSTM, and MGU—to accurately forecast Panasonic 18650PF cell temperatures under diverse conditions. The work set a new benchmark for machine-learning-driven battery thermal management, delivering superior accuracy, computational efficiency, and actionable insights for electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and high-performance electronics.
